Identity Search

& Purpose

Purpose of life :

1. Volunteering

Helping others gives deeper meaning to life.

2. Explore interests

Find 3 things that bring you joy and invest time in them.

3. Experiment

Try new hobbies and skills to understand yourself better.


Pressure when finding the right path :

1. No “perfect timing”

Stop comparing your pace with others.

2. Value of failure

Failure is learning—not the end.

3. Parental expectation

Don't live only to fulfil others dreams.


For we are God's workmanship, created in Christ Jesus to do good works.

Ephesians 2:10
