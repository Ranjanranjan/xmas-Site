Career & Business

Reducing work stress :

1. Prioritise

Focus on the 20% tasks that create 80% of your results.

2. Work-life balance

Avoid thinking about work once you finish your office hours.

3. Learn to say “No”

Protect your time by avoiding unnecessary overload.


Success goals :

1. SMART goals

Set goals that are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant & Time-bound.

2. Learn continuously

Spend at least 15 minutes daily learning something new.

3. Seek mentorship

Get guidance to avoid mistakes and grow faster.
