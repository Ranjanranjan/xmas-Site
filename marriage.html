Marriage Life & Partnership

Communication with understanding :

1. Use “I feel…” statements

Express feelings without blaming your partner.

2. Talk openly

Spend at least 10 minutes daily sharing thoughts.

3. Listen without judgement

Allow your spouse to speak freely and calmly.


Building intimacy :

4. Spend quality time together

Build emotional closeness through shared moments.

5. Do thoughtful acts of care

Small gestures make big emotional impact.

6. Appreciate your spouse genuinely

Express gratitude for their presence and efforts.


Each one of you must love his wife as he loves himself,
and the wife must respect her husband.
